Cookie Policy

In short: this website does not use analytics, advertising or tracking cookies. The only thing it stores in your browser is a record of your cookie consent choice.

What cookies are

"Cookies" is a general term for small pieces of data a website saves in your browser. Modern browsers also support a similar mechanism called local storage. Both are governed in the UK by the Privacy and Electronic Communications Regulations (PECR) and, where personal data is involved, by UK GDPR.

What this site stores

First-party storage (set by this site)

  • radiance_cookie_consent — local storage entry. Records the choice you make in the consent banner ("accepted" or "rejected") together with a timestamp, so the banner does not reappear on every visit.
    Strictly necessary; not used for tracking. Persists until you clear your browser storage.

Third-party loads

  • Google Fonts — the site loads two typefaces (Cormorant Garamond and Jost) from fonts.googleapis.com and fonts.gstatic.com. This is for typography and does not set tracking cookies. Google may receive your IP address as part of the request, as is the case for any web font request; nothing else is shared by this site.

What this site does not use

For clarity, the following are not present on this website:

  • No analytics — there is no Google Analytics, Plausible, Fathom, Hotjar or similar.
  • No advertising or remarketing tags — no Google Ads, Meta/Facebook Pixel, TikTok Pixel, LinkedIn Insight or similar.
  • No tracking pixels of any kind.
  • No social media share or "like" buttons that load third-party trackers.
  • No session-recording or heatmap tools.

Managing your choice

The consent banner is shown on your first visit. Once you choose "Accept" or "Reject", the choice is remembered and the banner does not reappear.

To reset your choice and see the banner again, clear local storage for this site in your browser, or use a private/incognito window.

Browser-level cookie controls

You can also block or delete cookies directly in your browser settings. Most major browsers publish guidance:

  • Google Chrome
  • Safari (Mac)
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Microsoft Edge

Changes to this policy

If anything changes about how the site uses cookies or storage, this policy will be updated and the date below revised.
